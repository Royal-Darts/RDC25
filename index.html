MVP Formula Definition

MVP Score =

(NormAvg × 0.35) + (NormWinRate × 0.25) + (NormLegsWon × 0.20) + (NormHighFinish × 0.05) + (TeamSuccessScore × 0.15)

Where:

  • NormAvg = Player’s 3DAvg ÷ Highest 3DAvg
  • NormWinRate = (LegsWon ÷ LegsPlayed) ÷ Highest WinRate
  • NormLegsWon = LegsWon ÷ Highest LegsWon
  • NormHighFinish = HighFinish ÷ Highest HighFinish
  • TeamSuccessScore = 1 - ((TeamRank-1)/(TotalTeams-1)), i.e. 1 for 1st, 0 for last (scaled)

Note: Minimum 2 matches played for MVP eligibility. Data auto-normalized per tournament.
